Isha Volunteer Intro Kit

A compact space to access all key materials to introduce Inner Engineering, Miracle of Mind, Sadhguru, and Isha. Tap any section below to open the resource you need in the moment.

Section 01 Introduce Sadhguru & Isha Key points and resources to present Sadhguru and Isha Foundation clearly and simply. Section 02 Introduce Inner Engineering Overview of Inner Engineering, what it offers, and who it is for. Section 03 Share Your Experience Prompts to help you share your own journey authentically and effectively. Section 04 Share Others’ Experiences Stories, sharings, and testimonials you can use based on who you are speaking with. Section 05 Referral & Discounts How to offer referral links or discounts for Inner Engineering Online. Section 08 Sadhguru in Malaysia 2026 Details and links connected to Sadhguru’s upcoming visit & Ecstasy of Enlightenment.
